\section{Projects}

Identified 4 genes as molecular markers of COVID-19 using permutation feature importance.\\
\vspace{5pt}
\normalsize \textbf{Vehicle Coupon Classification Model}\\
Used \textbf{Pandas}, \textbf{Matplotlib}, and \textbf{Seaborn} to perform exploratory visualizations on a UCI ML Vehicle Coupon dataset. Trained the \textbf{XGBoost} method with feature selection, hyperparameter tuning, and cross-validation in \textbf{Scikit-learn}, with a maximal F1-score of 0.81 for coupon acceptances.\\
\vspace{5pt}
\normalsize \textbf{Car-Obstacle Game with NEAT AI}\\
Designed a game with \textbf{Python} and \textbf{Pygame} with the aim of navigating a car through a series of obstacles. Built a robust AI using the NeuroEvolution of Augmenting Topologies (\textbf{NEAT}) genetic algorithm that successfully passes all 15 of the varying patterns of obstacles.\\
\vspace{5pt}
\normalsize \textbf{NBA Draft Scraper and Visualizations}\\
Scraped top rookies’ stats from the past 20 NBA drafts from Basketball Reference using \textbf{Beautiful Soup}. Processed data with feature engineering and investigated several analysis questions using \textbf{Matplotlib}, \textbf{Seaborn}, and \textbf{Plotly} visualizations along with Linear Discriminant Analysis from \textbf{Scikit-learn}.\\
